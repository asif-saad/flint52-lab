Who We Are

A research lab built for Bangla language AI

FLINT52 — Fine-tuned Language & Intelligent Neural Technology Lab — is an independent AI research group based in Bangladesh, dedicated to advancing NLP and machine learning for the Bangla language.

Mission

Why FLINT52 exists

Bangla is spoken by over 230 million people, yet it remains significantly under-resourced in modern AI and NLP research. Most large language models are trained predominantly on English data, leaving Bangla speakers with tools that perform poorly on their language.

FLINT52 was founded to close that gap — by building datasets, models, benchmarks, and open research that put Bangla at the centre of the work, not as an afterthought.

Values

What guides our work

Open Science

We publish our datasets, model weights, and code openly wherever possible, so others can build on our work.

Language Equity

Every language deserves quality AI tools. We focus specifically on Bangla to reduce the global NLP resource gap.

Rigorous Evaluation

We hold our models to high standards, building benchmarks that measure real-world usefulness — not just leaderboard scores.

Collaborative Research

We actively seek partnerships with universities, industry, and independent researchers across South Asia and beyond.
