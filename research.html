What We Study

Research at the frontier of Bangla language AI

Our work spans four interconnected research areas — all aimed at making AI more capable, equitable, and useful for Bangla speakers worldwide.

Area 01

Bangla NLP

We develop core NLP components for Bangla — a morphologically rich, fusional language with significant dialectal variation. Our work covers the full pipeline from raw text to structured linguistic representations.

  • Subword tokenisation tailored to Bangla morphology
  • Part-of-speech tagging for standard and colloquial Bangla
  • Named entity recognition across news, social media, and literature
  • Dependency parsing and syntactic analysis
  • Sentiment analysis and opinion mining

Area 02

LLM Fine-tuning

We investigate parameter-efficient methods for adapting large pre-trained language models to Bangla tasks — achieving strong performance without requiring full model retraining.

  • LoRA and QLoRA adaptation for Bangla instruction following
  • Instruction-tuning dataset curation and quality filtering
  • Continual pre-training on Bangla corpora
  • RLHF and preference alignment for Bangla chat models
  • Multilingual fine-tuning with Bangla as primary target

Area 03

Low-resource Learning

Many Bangla NLP tasks lack sufficient annotated data. We develop techniques that allow models to learn robustly from small datasets, noisy labels, and cross-lingual supervision.

  • Few-shot and zero-shot learning for Bangla classification tasks
  • Cross-lingual transfer from high-resource languages
  • Data augmentation via back-translation and paraphrasing
  • Semi-supervised and self-supervised learning approaches
  • Active learning for efficient annotation of Bangla corpora

Area 04

Evaluation & Benchmarks

Reliable progress requires reliable measurement. We design evaluation frameworks and public benchmarks that rigorously test Bangla language models across diverse tasks and domains.

  • Multi-task benchmark suites covering NLU and NLG
  • Human evaluation protocols for generative Bangla models
  • Domain-specific evaluation — news, healthcare, legal, informal text
  • Bias and fairness auditing for Bangla language models
  • Reproducibility standards and open leaderboards
