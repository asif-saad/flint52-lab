Building Bangla Language Models and AI that speaks natively.

FLINT52 — Fine-tuned Language & Intelligent Neural Technology Lab — conducts open research on language models, fine-tuning, RLHF, and neural architectures for Bangla and other low-resource languages of South Asia. Advancing NLP for underrepresented communities through cutting-edge AI research.

What we work on

Core NLP & AI Research Areas

Four interconnected pillars of natural language processing and artificial intelligence research, grounded in the needs of Bangla and low-resource language communities worldwide.

01

Bangla Language Modeling

Pre-training and fine-tuning advanced large language models (LLMs) for Bangla — including text classification, generation, machine translation, summarization, and question answering at scale.

Pre-trainingLLMNLPBangla
02

Fine-tuning & Alignment

Advanced instruction tuning, reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF), and direct preference optimization (DPO) methods to align language models with human values and safety standards in Bangla and multilingual contexts.

RLHFDPOAlignmentFine-tuning
03

Neural Architecture Research

Research into efficient transformer variants, novel attention mechanisms, and optimized neural architectures suited to low-compute settings common in university research labs across Bangladesh and South Asia.

TransformersEfficiencyArchitectureDeep Learning
04

Applied AI for Bangladesh

Real-world applications of machine learning and AI — healthcare NLP systems, legal document analysis, agricultural advisory systems, and educational tools designed for Bangla speakers.

HealthcareEducationSocial GoodApplied AI
